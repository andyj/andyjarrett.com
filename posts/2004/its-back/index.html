Its back.......

Thats right, we all though it had dies quietly in background but good ol faithful is back and coming to a operating system near you soon. I am of course talking about the Netscape browser. Incase you havent heard the news browser is rising from the ashes with help from Firefox. Mozillazine has an article taking a first look at Netscape new offering, and from the screen shot its looking heavily skinned but very polished. The good news for us developers is that its based on Firefox so at least its not another browser to download, install and check the site in
